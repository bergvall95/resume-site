Hi! I'm Alex, a developer thats just starting his journey, feel free to contact me on @linkedIn!

I started life off in a small town in south-east Texas. I spent my summers in Sweden, visiting my fathers family and eating (and loving) weird things like pickled herring and Kalles Kaviar.

Years later, I began my journey into tech as a Biomedical Engineering major at the University of Texas. The Biomedical field had a vast breadth of challenging material, that allowed me to learn and explore all kinds of fields in my time there.

Early on in the curriculum I was doing complex numerical analysis with Matlab, which was interesting, but far above my level as someone with no coding experience. I thought that I had no chance in the coding world if that was where beginners started.

Fortunately, I ended up in an embedded systems class that had me design the beginning levels of a simple video game. This is where I discovered my love for design.
